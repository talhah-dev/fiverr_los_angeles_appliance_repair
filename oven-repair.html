Common Oven Problems

Oven Problems We Repair Every Day

If baking results are inconsistent or the oven won’t heat, we’ll diagnose the cause and restore safe, accurate performance.

Oven Not Heating

Failed heating elements, igniters, thermostats, or wiring can prevent proper heating. We test and replace faulty components.

Uneven Temperature / Overheating

Temperature sensors, calibration, convection fans, and door seals can cause uneven cooking. We restore accuracy and stability.

Door Won’t Close or Seal

Broken hinges, worn gaskets, or misalignment can leak heat and reduce performance. We repair seals and hardware.

Control Panel / Error Codes

If buttons don’t respond or error codes appear, we inspect control boards, sensors, and wiring to resolve the issue.
