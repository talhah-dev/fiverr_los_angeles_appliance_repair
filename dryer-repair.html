Dryer Safety

Burning Smell or Overheating? Stop and Call.

Overheating dryers and clogged vents can be a fire risk. If you notice a burning smell or excessive heat, stop using the dryer and contact a technician.

Quick safety steps:

Turn off the dryer and unplug if safe to do so.

Clean the lint filter and check vent airflow.

Call for a diagnostic to prevent further damage.
