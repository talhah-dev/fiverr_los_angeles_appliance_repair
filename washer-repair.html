Common Washer Problems

Washer Not Working? We Fix These Issues Fast.

If your washer is leaking, not spinning, or leaving clothes soaking wet, our technicians can diagnose the issue quickly and recommend the best fix.

Call Now

Washer Leaking Water

Leaks can come from hoses, door seals, pumps, or drainage lines. We’ll locate the source and repair it properly.

Not Spinning or Agitating

Often caused by a worn belt, motor coupling, lid switch, or control board issue. We’ll diagnose and fix the root cause.

Not Draining Properly

Clogged filters, broken pumps, or blocked drain lines can stop draining. We’ll restore proper flow and prevent future backups.

Loud Noises or Shaking

Banging, grinding, or excessive vibration may indicate suspension, bearings, or an uneven load issue. We’ll stabilize and repair it.

Error Codes & Power Issues

If your washer won’t turn on or shows an error code, we’ll inspect sensors, wiring, and electronics to resolve it.

Quick Tips Before You Call

Confirm the washer is plugged in and the breaker hasn’t tripped.

Check if the load is balanced and the door/lid fully closes.

If you see an error code, take a photo — it helps diagnosis.
