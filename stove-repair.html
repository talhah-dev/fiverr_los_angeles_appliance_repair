Common Stove Problems

Stove Problems We Repair (Gas & Electric)

Whether you have a gas range with ignition problems or an electric cooktop with a dead burner, we diagnose and repair the issue quickly.

Burner Clicking or Won’t Ignite (Gas)

Clogged burner ports, faulty igniters, or moisture buildup can cause clicking and ignition failures. We clean, test, and repair ignition safely.

Uneven Heat or Weak Flame

Weak or uneven heat may come from burner caps, valves, regulators, or electrical control issues. We restore stable heat output.

Electric Burner Not Heating

Failed elements, infinite switches, wiring, or control boards can stop an electric burner from heating. We diagnose and replace faulty parts.

Knobs, Controls, or Display Not Working

If controls are unresponsive, we inspect switches, wiring, and electronic components to restore safe operation.

Stove Safety

Smell Gas or Hear Hissing? Stop and Call.

If you smell gas, turn off the stove immediately. Do not use flames or electrical switches. Leave the area and contact the gas company if necessary, then call for service.

Turn off burners and the gas supply valve if safe.

Ventilate by opening doors/windows if possible.

Call for professional diagnosis before using it again.
