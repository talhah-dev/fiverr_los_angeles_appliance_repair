Refrigerator Problems We Repair Every Day

Fridge Not Cooling

Causes can include dirty condenser coils, a failing compressor, faulty thermostat, or evaporator fan issues. We pinpoint and fix it.

Freezer Too Warm or Frosting Up

Defrost heater, defrost thermostat, and airflow problems can cause frost buildup or warm temps. We repair the defrost system.

Ice Maker Not Working

Water inlet valves, clogged filters, frozen lines, or faulty sensors can stop ice production. We restore proper ice-making.

Leaking Water

Leaks may come from clogged defrost drains, water lines, or damaged fittings. We locate the leak and fix it properly.

Food Safety

Fridge Not Cooling? Act Quickly to Protect Your Food.

A warm refrigerator can spoil food fast. If your fridge is above safe temperature, call for service as soon as possible.

Quick actions you can take:

Keep doors closed to maintain cool air as long as possible.

Move perishables to a cooler with ice if temps rise.

Call for diagnosis to prevent compressor damage.
